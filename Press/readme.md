# Prototype — KyKyKy


## Paper Prototyping


To help visualize and make sense of our story world, we created a set of **drawn paper prototypes** characters and objects placed and moved to help visualize the story.


Each character was represented:


- **Rokhy** -> the baby
- **Lucky** -> the English Bulldog
- **Cheeky** -> the Maine Coon


Key objects were included too: the robot-cleaner, Lucky's and Cheeky's food bowl, and Rokhy's crib.


---


## Stop Motion Video


The paper pieces were moved and staged to create a **stop motion video**, helping us walk through the key moments of the story and explain it clearly for the presentation: the first encounters, the rising tensions, and the turning point when Lucky falls ill.
