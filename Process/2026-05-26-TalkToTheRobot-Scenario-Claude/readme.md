# Kykyky! — Level Design Summary (Levels 1–3)

**Team:** Zainab Ouriachi · Maria Sarafi · Rokhy Niang
**Context:** Cohabitation / Maison collective
**Core principle:** one gesture (tap = care) · sound + movement · no words · aimed at children 4–8

---

## Level 1 — Rokhy & Lucky

### Part A — Lucky comforts the baby

daytime, baby cries in rocking chair → tap (steady/repeated) → Lucky advances: woof + claw-clicks, cry softens → stop 2s → gentle panic, louder cry → resume tap → Lucky reaches her → rocks her → giggle → rest & watch

### Transition

day → evening → night (color shift) → Lucky pads to bed, claw-clicks fade → quiet → morning rises → baby a few months older, now able to crawl

### Part B — the baby comforts Lucky

Lucky asleep, nightmare, whimpering → tap → baby crawls toward him, whimpering softens → stop 2s → nightmare worsens gently → resume tap → baby reaches him → settles against his side → quiet breathing → rest & watch

---

## Level 2 — Cheeky arrives

Cheeky enters as a roaming disruptor — the calm of Level 1 keeps getting broken.

### Scene 1 — Observation: the disruption

Cheeky arrives — meow + hum of the robot-cleaner throne → bumps into her throne → crosses the room, her own claw-clicks → squeezes between Lucky & Rokhy, separating them in the bed → reaches the dog's bowl → calm repeatedly disturbed → child taps as before, but tapping does **not** reach Cheeky yet → the unease is the point

### Scene 2 — Care loop: Rokhy the peacemaker

Cheeky eats from the bowl (lapping/crunching) → Lucky whines strongly → tap (multiple, like L1) → Rokhy moves between cat and dog, babbles at Cheeky to stop → stop 2s → Lucky whines harder / Cheeky keeps eating → resume tap → Rokhy reaches them → Cheeky stops eating and backs off → Lucky barks happily → Cheeky leaves on her robot-cleaner (hum fading) → end of scene

*Cheeky is chased off here, not yet won over.*

### Scene 3 — The turn: Cheeky learns to care (bridge to Level 3)

Lucky lies sick, whining → Rokhy beside him trying to comfort him, but he keeps whining → Cheeky circles on her robot-cleaner (hum) → tap → Cheeky steps **down** off the robot → tap (multiple) → Cheeky crosses toward Lucky → his whining softens as she nears → stop 2s → whining rises again → resume tap → Cheeky reaches him → she comforts him → first **purr** (Cheeky has softened) → the three rest together, calm side by side → bridge into Level 3

*The purr is a new sound that appears only now — the wordless sign the grump has changed. The child makes the turn happen: tapping is what brings Cheeky down off her throne.*

---

## Level 3 — All together (the KyKyKy team)

### Transition

day → evening → night (color shift) → quiet → morning rises → another year has passed → Rokhy is bigger again, now **walking** → small wordless signs of time in the room (a taller plant, more toys on the floor)

### Final scene — everyone arrives at once

morning, Rokhy toddles in → tap → **both** Lucky and Cheeky come running from opposite sides → they meet her in the middle → their sounds overlap into one happy chorus (giggle + bark + purr) → they tumble and play together → all three pile onto Cheeky's robot-cleaner → the hum, once a disruption, is now a happy ride → they ride around together → end

*The throne that caused every fight in Level 2 now carries all three — the object that divided them unites them. Love is shown through sounds in harmony and everyone arriving at once. No hearts, no symbols.*

---

## Why it works

- **One gesture only:** tap = care, all the way through.
- **Sound + movement together:** the cry/whine/meow calls, the movement (an approach, a crawl, stepping off the throne) catches the eye.
- **L1 mirrors itself:** baby cared for, then caring back — care flows both ways.
- **L2 escalates the role:** baby as peacemaker between cohabitants; then the child draws even the grump into caring.
- **L3 unites the mechanic:** the tap that always brought one-to-one now brings everyone at once.
- **Growth marks time:** tiny → crawling → walking, across the three levels.
- **New sounds mark change:** giggle = comfort received · purr = Cheeky caring · chorus = the team.
